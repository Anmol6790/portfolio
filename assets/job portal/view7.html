Machine Learning Engineer

Company: Tesla

Location: Palo Alto, CA

Experience: 3+ years

Salary: $140,000 - $160,000/year

Job Description

As a Machine Learning Engineer at Tesla, you will work on innovative projects that drive the future of autonomous driving and intelligent vehicle systems. You will develop machine learning models and algorithms to enhance the capabilities of Tesla products.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement machine learning algorithms for real-time applications.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate ML models into products.
  • Analyze large datasets to extract meaningful insights and improve models.
  • Monitor and optimize the performance of machine learning models.
  • Stay updated with the latest advancements in ML and AI technologies.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  • Strong programming skills in Python, TensorFlow, or PyTorch.
  • Experience with data analysis and statistical modeling.
  • Familiarity with software development methodologies and tools.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work in a team.

How to Apply

If you're interested in this position, please send your resume and cover letter to hr@tesla.com.
