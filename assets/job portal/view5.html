UX/UI Designer

Company: Microsoft

Location: Redmond, WA

Experience: 3+ years

Salary: $90,000 - $110,000/year

Job Description

As a UX/UI Designer at Microsoft, you will create exceptional user experiences across our products. You will collaborate with product managers and developers to design intuitive interfaces that enhance user engagement and satisfaction.

Responsibilities

  • Design user interfaces for web and mobile applications.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to gather insights.
  • Work closely with product teams to define user requirements.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups.
  • Stay current with industry trends and best practices in UX/UI design.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, or a related field.
  • Proven experience in UX/UI design with a strong portfolio.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Adobe XD, Sketch, or Figma.
  • Strong understanding of user-centered design principles.
  • Excellent communication skills and the ability to work collaboratively.

How to Apply

If you're interested in this position, please send your resume and cover letter to hr@microsoft.com.
