Data Scientist

Company: Facebook

Location: Menlo Park, CA

Experience: 4+ years

Salary: $130,000 - $150,000/year

Job Description

As a Data Scientist at Facebook, you will analyze large datasets to derive actionable insights and improve decision-making processes. You will collaborate with product teams to enhance user experience and drive business growth through data-driven strategies.

Responsibilities

  • Analyze complex datasets and interpret results to inform product strategies.
  • Develop predictive models and machine learning algorithms.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify opportunities for data-driven improvements.
  • Communicate findings and recommendations to stakeholders effectively.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest data science technologies and methodologies.

Requirements

  • Master's degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python or R.
  • Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).
  • Strong analytical skills and experience with statistical modeling.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.

How to Apply

If you're interested in this position, please send your resume and cover letter to hr@facebook.com.
