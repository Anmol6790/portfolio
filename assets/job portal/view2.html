Backend Developer

Company: Amazon

Location: Seattle, WA

Experience: 2+ years

Salary: $110,000 - $130,000/year

Job Description

As a Backend Developer at Amazon, you will work on server-side logic and database management to ensure high performance and responsiveness of applications. You will collaborate with front-end developers to integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement scalable backend systems and APIs.
  • Maintain and improve existing systems and services.
  • Collaborate with front-end developers to integrate user-facing elements.
  • Conduct code reviews and provide feedback to team members.
  • Troubleshoot and debug applications to optimize performance.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • Experience with backend programming languages such as Java, Python, or Node.js.
  • Familiarity with databases like MySQL, MongoDB, or PostgreSQL.
  • Understanding of RESTful APIs and microservices architecture.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a team.

How to Apply

If you're interested in this position, please send your resume and cover letter to hr@amazon.com.
