DevOps Engineer

Company: Oracle

Location: Austin, TX

Experience: 4+ years

Salary: $125,000 - $145,000/year

Job Description

As a DevOps Engineer at Oracle, you will work to streamline our development processes and enhance the efficiency of our operations. You will collaborate with development and IT teams to implement best practices in automation, monitoring, and deployment.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement CI/CD pipelines for applications.
  • Automate infrastructure provisioning and configuration management.
  • Monitor system performance and troubleshoot issues proactively.
  • Collaborate with software engineers to improve development workflows.
  • Ensure security best practices are followed in all deployments.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Experience with cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or GCP.
  • Proficiency in scripting languages such as Python, Bash, or Ruby.
  • Knowledge of containerization technologies like Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

How to Apply

If you're interested in this position, please send your resume and cover letter to hr@oracle.com.
